Urban proTrade GmbH Established in 1986

For over 20 years, Axel Urban has been devoted to the Ferrari myth with great passion and has been internationally acknowledged as true specialist of classic Ferrari’s. The company primarily sell cars of their own stock, which assures our clients that we know all the ‘Ins and Outs’ on each of our cars. We have owned many of the greatest Ferrari cars on earth – and of course we regret every single one we sold!

Axel and his brother Klaus have moved their bussines to Munich in 2005 and have always a great selection of special Ferrari's for you to enjoy ! We look forward to your visit.
